About Us

Hi! Here we are. Vlad and Dasha. An extraordinary couple from Ukraine, whose relationship started from the weirdest circumstances. This is our place, where we will be able to keep our beautiful memories and emotions.

We adore traveling, especially hiking in the mountains, racoons and cats. At this moment we have been dating for 9 months and we are eager to continue this journey toghether.

We bonded over this passion, so we would like to save, cherish and recall all our experiences, our love story and emotions one day.

Our stories are more than just pictures and words; they are memories of sunsets atop mountains, the thrill of hidden trails, the difficulties that may arouse along the way and the joy of trying something new together. Despite the difficult circumstances in our country we are trying to take as much out of this life as possible. We are brave enough to step outside, explore the unknown, and create our own extraordinary moments. Live is worth living as long as we have places to go to and each other to share it all with :>
